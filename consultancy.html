Consultancy & Project Management

At Ikaisoft, we provide expert consultancy services that empower students, professionals, educational institutions, and businesses to achieve their academic, career, and organizational goals. Our experienced consultants offer personalized guidance in academic planning, career development, interview preparation, project management, and institutional partnerships. By combining industry expertise with practical strategies, we help clients make informed decisions, overcome challenges, and achieve measurable success. Whether you're preparing for placements, planning a career transition, managing complex projects, or seeking strategic academic support, Ikaisoft is committed to delivering reliable, result-oriented consultancy tailored to your unique needs.

Academic Guidance

Expert counseling to help students make informed academic decisions and prepare for long-term success.

  • Career path assessment
  • Course & university selection guidance
  • Academic planning & strategy
  • Scholarship & funding assistance
Interview Preparation

Structured coaching sessions to prepare you for technical, HR, and behavioral interviews with confidence.

  • Technical mock interview sessions
  • Detailed interviewer feedback
  • HR & behavioral interview coaching
  • Communication & personality development
  • Group discussion (GD) practice
Job-Ready Consultation

Build a strong professional profile with practical career guidance and job search strategies.

  • Resume & portfolio building
  • LinkedIn profile optimization
  • Job search strategy
  • Application planning
  • Networking & soft-skills coaching
Project Management Services

Professional project planning and execution support for students, institutions, startups, and businesses.

  • Technical project coordination

  • Agile/Scrum & Gantt-based project tracking
Academic Activities & Competitions
